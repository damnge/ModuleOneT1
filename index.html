Dog Missing!

Please help us find Mounty

On Monday the 15th August 2022 Monty was out for a walk with his Dad, Monty had been playing with a stick then it was time to head home, his Dad put him on the lead to cross the roads and onto the next bit of green space where a normally sensible Monty was let off again.

Monty is a timid boy anyway, wary of strangers and a good deal of noises, now he'd also had the traumatic experience of being hit by a car. His Parents, family and friends searched for hours that night, fearing the worst that he was injured or even dead somewhere near to where he was hit.

If you may know anything please do not hesitate to contact us.
